# Scope

## In Scope (V1)
- Ingest public opportunities from X.
- Classify intent and rank opportunity.
- Propose replies and daily posts.
- Validate output for brand and anti-cringe rules.
- Queue, approve, publish, and track metrics.
- Monitor priority leads.
- Seed ideas from Telegram content.

## Out of Scope (V1)
- Autonomous posting without safety guardrails.
- Multi-platform publishing beyond X and Telegram seed.
- Paid ads management.
- Full CRM automation.
