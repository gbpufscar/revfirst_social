# Growth Strategy

## ICP
- Founder-led B2B teams
- Product and growth operators
- Technical decision makers

## Positioning
RevFirst_Social prioritizes high-signal conversations over vanity posting volume.

## Conversation Strategy
- Prioritize threads with explicit pain or decision intent.
- Reply with practical, specific guidance.
- Avoid broad thought-leadership filler.
- Escalate to DM only when clear value is established.

## Feedback Loop
Use weekly metrics to rebalance opportunity thresholds, topics, and response style.
