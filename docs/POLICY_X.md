# X Policy

## Account Safety
- Respect rate limits and posting windows.
- Do not automate aggressive follow/unfollow patterns.
- Avoid repetitive reply templates.

## Content Safety
- No harassment, spam, or misleading claims.
- No low-context hijacking of sensitive topics.
- Keep replies relevant to original thread intent.

## Operational Policy
- Approval gate required for publish actions in V1.
- Keep immutable logs for published content and metrics snapshots.
